About Our School

Shankardev Shishu Niketan, Bhuragaon stands as a beacon of quality education in the heart of Morigaon district. Established on January 31, 2004, through the dedicated efforts of local conscious people, our school has grown from humble beginnings to become a cornerstone of educational excellence in the region.

As a private Assamese medium school operated by Vidya Bharati Affiliated Shishu Shiksha Samiti, Assam, we are committed to providing holistic education that nurtures not just academic excellence but also character building and cultural values.

Our journey began with just three classes - Ankur, Mukul, and Pratham - opposite Bhuragaon Hindi School. Despite facing significant challenges including a devastating fire that destroyed our original site and necessitated two relocations, our determination remained unwavering.

Our Journey

Today, we are permanently established near the sacred Shiva temple at Bardobatup in Bhuragaon, symbolizing our deep roots in the community and our commitment to enduring educational service.

Our strategic location provides easy access from various parts of Bhuragaon, bordered by prominent landmarks including Bhuragaon College, Police Station, and Health Center, making us an integral part of the community's educational infrastructure.

Principal's Message

A Message from Our Principal

At Shankardev Shishu Niketan, we believe in the holistic development of every child. Our educational philosophy is rooted in the rich cultural heritage of Assam while embracing modern pedagogical methods to prepare our students for the challenges of tomorrow.

We foster an environment where students of all backgrounds can thrive, regardless of their religious or cultural affiliations. Our commitment to inclusive education ensures that every child receives the opportunity to discover their potential and develop into responsible citizens.

Through our comprehensive curriculum that includes moral education, physical fitness, cultural activities, and academic excellence, we strive to create well-rounded individuals who embody our motto - "Swayameva Mrigendrata" - becoming strong through their own qualities and actions.

- Shri Ram Chouhan, Principal

Academic Excellence

We offer a comprehensive educational program that combines academic rigor with cultural values, following the Vidya Bharati curriculum and SEBA syllabus in Assamese medium.

Core Curriculum

Mathematics, Science, Assamese, English, Social Studies, and Sanskrit following SEBA guidelines.

Value Education

Moral education, heroic stories, and personal hygiene to build character and life skills.

Cultural Activities

Music, yoga, physical education, and celebration of traditional festivals and cultural events.
